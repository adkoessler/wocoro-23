Scope

The workshop aims to gather researchers investigating collaborative applications in robotics. Topics such as human-robot interaction, dedicated sensing and control strategies, and industrial applications will be covered. Junior researchers are especially welcome, as presentations and demonstrations of the UR Collaborate Tour will give them a broad view of the field of collaborative robotics.
